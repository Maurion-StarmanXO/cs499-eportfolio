Professional Self-Assessment

Completing the Computer Science program and developing this ePortfolio has allowed me to measure how much I have grown both technically and professionally. Throughout the program, I developed stronger skills in programming, problem solving, system design, databases, debugging, and communication.

One of the most valuable areas of growth was collaboration. Many courses required discussion, feedback, and project communication. These experiences taught me how to work with others, accept constructive criticism, and contribute effectively within a team environment.

I also improved my ability to communicate with stakeholders. Several projects required translating technical requirements into language that users and nontechnical audiences could understand. This skill helped me connect business goals with technical solutions.

My understanding of algorithms and data structures also expanded significantly. I learned how efficiency, logical design, and organization directly affect software performance and maintainability.

Through database and software engineering projects, I gained experience with backend systems, MongoDB, Python, dashboards, debugging, testing, and system planning. These projects demonstrated how multiple technologies must work together to create useful software solutions.

Security awareness also became an important part of my development. I learned to consider data protection, access control, reliability, and secure design practices when creating software systems.

Overall, this ePortfolio represents my academic growth, technical progress, and readiness to continue building my career in systems administration, DevOps, cloud technologies, automation, and security.
